# About Us

## Our Story

Last Unique Touch started from a simple vision: to make luxury furniture and event equipment rental in Kuwait a transparent, easy, and reliable experience. Our founder noticed that the market suffered from a lack of price transparency, difficulty in booking products, and a shortage of modern luxury options.

We faced many challenges in the beginning — from building a wide product base to earning customer trust in a market dominated by direct communication. But we believed that a transparent digital platform was the future.

So we offered a solution that combines luxury with simplicity: an integrated digital platform where every product is displayed with a clear price, with instant booking capability, 3D product previews, and real-time availability checking.

Today, we proudly serve more than 1,000 happy clients, equip more than 2,000 successful events, with a base exceeding 500 carefully curated luxury products. We continue to grow and develop to be the premier destination for luxury furniture rental in Kuwait.

## Our Vision & Values

We believe that luxury events start from the small details. Therefore, we commit to four core values in everything we offer:

- **Quality First**: We carefully curate every piece, from design to maintenance, to ensure it reaches you in the best condition.
- **Full Transparency**: Clear prices with no surprises. What you see is what you pay — daily rental rate and security deposit together.
- **Speed & Flexibility**: Quick booking through the website, flexible rental by day or week, and professional delivery on time.
- **Modern Luxury**: Modern designs with a luxurious touch, selected from the finest global and local brands.
