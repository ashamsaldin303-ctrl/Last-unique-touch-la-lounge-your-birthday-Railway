# Refund Policy

## 1. Introduction

At Last Unique Touch, we strive for complete customer satisfaction. This refund policy outlines the terms and processes for booking cancellations, refunds, and security deposits. By using our services, you agree to this policy.

## 2. Security Deposit

### 2.1 Deposit Amount
A security deposit is paid with each booking as a guarantee to protect the products. The deposit amount varies by product and is clearly displayed at booking.

### 2.2 When It's Refunded
The deposit is fully refunded after returning the products in good condition, after inspection and verification of no damage.

### 2.3 Refund Timeframe
The deposit refund process takes 7-14 business days from the date of product pickup.

### 2.4 When Deductions Apply
Deductions from the deposit may apply in the following cases:
- **Product damage**: Repair or replacement costs
- **Product loss**: Full product value
- **Late return**: Late fees for each day of delay
- **Dirty return**: Additional cleaning fees

## 3. Booking Cancellation

The refund percentage varies based on cancellation timing:

### 3.1 Cancellation 7+ days before start date
**Full refund (100%)** of the rental amount (excluding processing fees if any).

### 3.2 Cancellation 3-7 days before start date
**50% refund** of the rental amount.

### 3.3 Cancellation less than 3 days before start date
**No refund (0%)** of the rental amount.

### 3.4 After rental period starts
**No refund** — bookings cannot be cancelled after the rental period has started.

## 4. Booking Modification

### 4.1 Date Modification
Rental dates can be modified up to 3 days before the start date, subject to availability. No fees for date modifications.

### 4.2 Quantity Modification
Quantity can be increased (subject to availability) or decreased up to 3 days before the start date. Financial differences are adjusted according to modified prices.

### 4.3 Product Modification
Products can be exchanged for another product up to 3 days before the start date, with payment or refund of price differences.

## 5. Damaged Products

### 5.1 Damage Assessment
Upon return, we carefully inspect the products. If damage is found, we assess repair or replacement costs.

### 5.2 Deposit Deduction
Repair or replacement costs are deducted from the security deposit. If costs exceed the deposit amount, the customer is responsible for the difference.

### 5.3 Repair Costs
Repair costs are determined based on specialized technical assessment. The customer has the right to request a detailed report of damages and costs.

## 6. Non-Return

### 6.1 Consumable Products
Consumable products (if any) are not returnable after use.

### 6.2 Special Customizations
Any special customizations (such as color changes or modifications) are not returnable.

## 7. Refund Process

### 7.1 Submitting a Cancellation Request
To cancel a booking, please contact us via the `/contact` page or by phone, mentioning your order number.

### 7.2 Request Review
We review cancellation requests within 1-2 business days and confirm the applicable refund percentage.

### 7.3 Refund Processing
After confirmation, we process the refund within 5-10 business days, depending on the original payment method.

## 8. Force Majeure

In cases of force majeure (weather conditions, natural disasters, government decisions, etc.), we may apply a special policy. Please contact us to discuss your individual case.
