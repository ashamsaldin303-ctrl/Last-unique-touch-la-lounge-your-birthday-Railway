# Privacy Policy

## 1. Introduction

Last Unique Touch is committed to protecting the privacy of its website visitors and customers. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information. By using our website, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information
When making a booking, we collect:
- Full name
- Phone number
- Email address
- Address
- City

### 2.2 Booking Information
- Booked product
- Rental period (start and end dates)
- Quantity
- Amount paid

### 2.3 Technical Information
- IP address
- Browser type
- Operating system
- Pages you visited
- Cookies

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:
- **Processing bookings**: Executing and confirming rental requests
- **Order communication**: Contacting you to confirm details or for inquiries
- **Invoicing & email**: Sending invoices, receipts, and booking confirmations
- **Improving services**: Analyzing website usage to improve experience
- **Legal compliance**: Meeting legal and regulatory requirements

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on:
- **Contract performance**: To process your bookings and provide services
- **Legal obligation**: To maintain financial and tax records
- **Your consent**: For using your data for marketing purposes (you can withdraw at any time)

## 5. Information Sharing

We do not sell your data to any third party. We may share it with:
- **Delivery companies**: For delivery and pickup execution
- **Payment gateway**: For processing financial transactions
- **n8n (internal automation)**: For sending Telegram notifications, invoice emails, and calendar reminders
- **Legal authorities**: When legally compelled by court order or competent authorities

## 6. Information Security

We apply strict security measures to protect your data:
- **HTTPS/SSL**: All connections are encrypted
- **Internal access restriction**: Data access is limited to authorized employees
- **Security logs**: We log security events for monitoring
- **Backup**: Regular data backups to prevent loss

## 7. Data Retention

We retain your data throughout your relationship with us, and thereafter for 5 years for accounting and tax purposes. After this period, we delete or anonymize the data securely.

## 8. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:
- **Right of access**: Request a copy of your data
- **Right to rectification**: Correct any inaccurate data
- **Right to erasure**: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations)
- **Right to restrict processing**: Request restriction of your data use
- **Right to data portability**: Receive your data in a portable format
- **Right to object**: Object to the processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us via the `/contact` page.

## 9. Cookies

We use cookies to improve your experience:
- **Essential cookies**: For the website to function properly (such as the shopping cart)
- **Analytical cookies**: To understand how the website is used

You can manage or disable cookies from your browser settings.

## 10. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us to have it removed.

## 11. International Transfers

Your data may be transferred outside the State of Kuwait in the following cases:
- **n8n**: The n8n server may be on an external server, but data is transmitted securely via HTTPS
- **Payment gateway**: Payment data is processed through an approved external provider

We ensure that these transfers are made according to required protection standards.

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will post changes on this page. We recommend reviewing this page periodically to stay informed.
